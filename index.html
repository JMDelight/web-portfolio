About me

I grew up in Boise Idaho, graduated at Boise High School in 2004 and then went on to study music at Boise State University. A few years into my program, life took me on a different path and I entered the work force. I held various jobs, many of which were in the food service industry. Some of my favorites included teaching the sport of fencing and being an in home caretaker. I eventually settled into management in the food service industry. As time passed I grew restless and decided I would go back to school and study to be a computer programmer.

My interests include gaming-computer, tabletop and board; Music-listening, playing and composing; fencing, hiking, cooking, juggling and reading.
